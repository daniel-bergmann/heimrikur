Heimrikur


Health, well-being and spirituality.



This is an Icelandic podcast and an online venue for exploring all modes of holistic thinking and well-being including herbalism, spirituality, philosophy and outdoor movement.

The two syllables of my name "Hin" and "Rik" individually stand for Heim in icelandic (or home english) and Rikur in icelandic (or rich in english).


So the name stands for something to the effect of:


The richness of home.


Home being a very abstract concept for me I take this to mean a sense of wellbeing and a familiarity within one's own being.


For me this is cultivation that encompasses all aspects of life and a constant work in progress. A goal that will never be fully grasped.


I therefore thought it would only be appropriate to name this website after the meaning of my name and my calling.


This website is above all an attempt to create a online venue (maybe even a virtual yoga room) where our minds meet to explore the horizon in cutting edge cultivation.


The process is threefold:


  1. To present body-mind cultivation in a non-dogmatic way.

  2. To go beyond the traditional seperation of mind and body.

  3. To interview as many people as I can and get as many potent perspectives and methods as possible.
